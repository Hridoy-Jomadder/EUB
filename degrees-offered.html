The EUB subscribes the idea of broadening the knowledge base of its graduates while emphasizing the specialized knowledge in their area of interests. Academic programs on offer are structured by subject areas scaffolding vertical integration of levels and horizontal integration of spread of knowledge and skills. The EUB is mindful of developing and delivering academic programs gradually along with the provision of adequate resources and facilities. In the first phase, EUB offers programs in Business Administration, English, Law, Tourism and Hospitality, Textile and Electronics and Electronic Engineering. THE EUB will offer programs in Civil Engineering, Architecture, Computer Science and Technology, Urban and Regional Planning, Environmental Studies, Pharmacy, Fashion Design, Agri-Business, Medicine and Surgery and Education. Special Care for English
The University will attach high priority to prepare students as competent professionals with high level of English communication skills. Special care for English is taken under the curriculum of the University (EUB) for the students who have lacking in the communication skills. Each student will require to attend enrichment courses in communication and life skills.
